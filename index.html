Privacy Policy for TailorPal Mobile Application

Welcome to TailorPal, a cutting-edge tailoring application developed and proudly operated by Walkaholic Enterprise. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we collect it, and how we use and protect that information.

1. Introduction:

TailorPal is an innovative mobile application designed to revolutionize the tailoring experience. Our app empowers tailors by providing advanced tools for adding and managing stitches, sending timely reminders for collection dates, and showcasing an array of fabrics with detailed measurement areas.

2. Information Collected:

To enhance your experience with TailorPal, we collect the following information directly from users through our secure registration forms:

  • Names: We collect your name to personalize your experience and address you appropriately.
  • Email Addresses: Your email address is essential for communication purposes, such as account-related notifications and updates.
  • Phone Numbers: We collect your phone number to facilitate communication and send important alerts or reminders.
  • Card Information: For users opting for premium features, we securely collect and store card information for seamless transactions.

3. How Information is Collected:

Information is gathered seamlessly through our user-friendly registration forms within the TailorPal application. We have implemented user-centric design to ensure a smooth and intuitive registration process.

4. Purpose of Data Collection:

The data we collect serves various purposes, including:

  • Improving Application Functionality: Your information helps us enhance and optimize the features and functionality of TailorPal.
  • Enhancing User Experience: Personalized data allows us to tailor the app experience to your preferences, providing a more enjoyable and efficient service.
  • Marketing Purposes: We may use aggregated and anonymized data for marketing analysis and to improve our services.

5. User Consent:

Before registering on TailorPal, every user is required to carefully read and accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Additionally, users have the option to request the deletion of their data at any time.

6. Data Security:

We take the security of your information seriously. All user data, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and card information, is encrypted and securely stored on our servers, utilizing industry-standard security measures.

7. Third-Party Sharing:

Rest assured, your data remains confidential. We do not share user data with any third party. Your privacy is our priority.

8. Country of Operation:

TailorPal is exclusively available and operates within the legal framework of Nigeria.

9. Company that Owns and Operates the App:

TailorPal is proudly owned and operated by Walkaholic Enterprise.

10. User Rights:

We respect your rights. All users have the right to request the deletion of their accounts, ensuring the removal of their data from our system.

11. Data Retention:

To respect your privacy, we do not retain user data beyond the necessary time required for the intended purposes.

12. Updates to the Privacy Policy:

Our commitment to your privacy includes keeping you informed. This privacy policy is subject to change, and any updates will be communicated to users.

13. Contact Information:
